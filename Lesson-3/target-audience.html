Audience Description

The target audience is between the ages of 20-75. They will access Desert Mountain Chamber from smart phones and at times from tablets, laptops, or desktop computers. The audience would include business owners and business leaders looking to join the chamber in order to network, promote their business and seek advocacy for their businesses. Tourists who are seeking to find out more about local and area attractions. Locals who are looking for the next community event. Families who are looking to find a beautiful, family centered community in which to raise their family.

Personas

Jillian

Jillian is the owner of a brand new Culver's franchise in the Mountain Desert area. She is 42 years old. She is married with three children. She enjoys living in the Mountain Desert area and is looking to become part of the Mountain Desert Chamber of Commerce. She would like to promote her new business and get to know other business owners in the area. She is often on the go and would most likely use her smart phone or tablet to acces business events on desertmountainchamber.com.

Michael and Lena

They are 32 years old and married with two children ages three and one. They are looking to relocate their family. They value a tightly knit community that with strong family values. They also enjoy being part of a community by attending community events. To get to know more about the Desert Mountain area they would visit the desertmountainchamber.com using their smart phones.

Scenarios

I am a new business owner who is seeking membership in the Desert Mountain Chamber of Commerce, where do I sign up? I heard that the Desert Mountain is a great place to vacation, how can I fine out more? Is there a community event going on this weekend? Are there any business networking groups in Desert Mountain and when, where and what time do they meet? We are looking for a family friendly place to relocate to, what is the Desert Mountain area like?
